The Most Beautiful Places in China

Guilin

Guilin is a world-famous scenic city, famous for its unrivalled karst landscape, exquisite mountains, limpid rivers, wired caves and beautiful rocks. Besides the unparallel scenery, the city also boasts its time-honored history.

Lijiang

The Old Town of Lijiang is China's best-preserved minority ancient town, was put in the list of the World's Relics by the UNESCO,and the only one among China's ancient towns without city walls. It's also famous for it's snow mountains.

Tibet

Tibet is known as the “Roof of the World”, the “Third Pole”, or the “Land of Snows”. It is one of the most renowned places in the world. It's the home of Buddhist Monasteries, the vast Himalayan Mountains,hairy yaks.
